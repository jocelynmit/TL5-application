WHY?

Today's a good time to do something for myself.

I've deliberated, hesitated, started and gave up on programming many, many times over the last decade.

I found out that the best way to overcome my fear of learning programming and rejection is to... announce my intention to apply to a bootcamp to many people. In fact, I was so convincing, I realised that it's too late to back out.

Surprisingly, no one questioned my capability to learn to program. And in trying very hard to piece together this particular webpage, I realised my love for tinkering with code and pixels would be more long-lived this time round.

And so I made a promise to myself - no matter the outcome of my application, I will pick up programming henceforth.
